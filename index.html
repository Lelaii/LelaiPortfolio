UX/UI Research

Study the process of systematically studying a product's target audience to understand their needs, behaviors, and pain points, gathering insights through various methods like interviews, surveys, and usability testing, to inform the design of a user interface (UI) that creates a positive user experience (UX) on a product or service.

UX/UI Designing

The process of creating digital products (like websites and apps) that are not only visually appealing but also intuitive and easy for users to interact with, focusing on both the overall user experience (UX) and the visual elements of the interface (UI) they see and interact with.

Front-End Development

The practice of creating the visual user interface of a website or application, including elements like buttons, text, and images, using languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which users directly interact with when they visit a site.
